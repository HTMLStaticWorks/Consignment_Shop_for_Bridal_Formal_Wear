Successful Consignment in 4 Steps

01

Initial Submission

Fill out our online submission form with photos and details about your gown (designer, size, year of purchase, and original price).

02

Detailed Review

Our expert team reviews your submission. If it matches our current inventory needs and quality standards, we'll invite you for an in-person assessment.

03

In-Person Inspection

Bring your dry-cleaned gown to our boutique. We'll inspect the condition, verify authenticity, and agree on a luxury listing price.

04

Listing & Sale

Your gown is professionally photographed and displayed in our boutique and online gallery. When it sells, you receive your commission!
