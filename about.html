Founded on the belief that luxury should be accessible and sustainable.

Luxe Bridal started as a small passion project in the heart of the city. We saw so many breathtaking gowns tucked away in closets after only one day of wear, and we knew there was a better way. We created a space where pre-loved couture can find a second life with a new bride.

Our mission is to provide an elevated, boutique experience for both consignors and buyers, ensuring that every dress continues its story of love and elegance.

The People Behind Luxe

Meet the Founders

Luxe Bridal was born from a simple conversation between two lifelong friends who shared a passion for couture and a deep concern for fashion waste. After witnessing thousands of designer gowns collect dust in closets, they knew the bridal industry needed a more elegant solution.

Today, their vision has grown into a boutique trusted by brides across the city — a place where pre-loved couture finds new purpose without losing its magic.
